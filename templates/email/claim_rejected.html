🔍 Common Requirements for Claim Approval

  • Business Documentation: Valid business license, registration, or tax ID
  • Proof of Ownership: Official documents showing you own or manage the business
  • Contact Information: Must match business records or website
  • Physical Location: Verifiable business address (not just P.O. Box)
  • Active Business: Evidence that the business is currently operating

✅ How to Resubmit Your Claim

  1. Review the requirements: Make sure you have all necessary documentation
  2. Gather additional proof: Business license, utility bills, website ownership, etc.
  3. Upload clear documents: Ensure all files are readable and current
  4. Provide accurate information: Double-check all contact details
  5. Submit your claim again: Use the same process with updated documentation
